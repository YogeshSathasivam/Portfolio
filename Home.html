About Me

An overview of my evolution from a graduate to a UI/UX professional.

I'm a UI/UX designer with a passion for crafting thoughtful, user-centered experiences. I bring a unique perspective to design, balancing creative problem-solving with a deep understanding of business objectives and logical decision-making.

Apart from designing, I like to attend design workshops for vibe check, learn new tools because I'm always eager to learn and grow.

When I'm not in front of a screen, you'll find me nurturing my plants, hitting the gym, or catching up with friends over long phone calls 😊
